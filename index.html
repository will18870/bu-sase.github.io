Who We Are

The Boston University Chapter of SASE was founded in 2013 to promote the success of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in the BU community. Our mission is to prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success, celebrate cultural diversity on campus, and provide opportunities to give back to our local community.
